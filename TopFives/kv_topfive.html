Top 5 Movies

Didi

Didi captured the familiar feelings of growing up, trying to fit in, and the experiences of being in an immigrant family. I appreciated this movie because there was nothing "special" to it, as in, there was no "multiverse" like Everything Everywhere All At Once; rather, it was all very realistic and raw enough to relate to on a deeper level.

  1. Didi [5★★★★★]
  2. Everything Everywhere All At Once [5★★★★★]
  3. Grave of the Fireflies [5★★★★★]
  4. Barbie [5★★★★★]
  5. Nobody Knows [4★★★★]
